About Us

The Foodie is here to help you cook delicious meals with less stress and more joy. We offer recipes and cooking advice for home cooks, by home cooks. Helping create “kitchen wins” is what we’re all about.

Tom Smith was a busy London executive when he became tired of the 9 to 5 hetic lifestyle. In 2019, Tom quit his job in JP Morgaan and moved home to Dublin to start his dream project.

History

Started in 2019 as a project to help the community share fresh new cooking ideas. The aim was to bring people together to share their knowledge and experiance.

Everyone is welcome at the Foodie's table: people of all races, religions, genders, sexual orientations, ages, backgrounds, and abilities. We strive to be a resource for every home cook, and we consciously work to make this inclusion felt in every part of the site, from the individuals we hire to the recipes we share.
